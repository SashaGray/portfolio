Dream your portfolio

The process

The "Dream your portfolio" assignment was about making a prototype of my own portfolio, using the program Adobe XD.
I tried making it as simple as possible with discret colors and not too much going on.
This program was easy to use, but as it was just for prototyping I couldn't link to anything, and I was very limited.

See my process (in danish) here!

The result

The result of my portfolio protoype was very simple and easy to use and navigate. I think that is important when making websites, because the easier it is to use the more people can and will use your website.
I made my prototype so I had a frontpage where you can navigate from there. Thoug I now think that i should have made a navigation bar with navigations to all the pages, follow you around my prototype.
